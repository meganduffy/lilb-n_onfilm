Expired I

Published by www.thephotoshop.ie. Some photographers seek out the unique aesthetic effects expired film can offer. Over time, the film’s emulsion degrades, leading to unpredictable colour shifts, increased grain, and a dreamier, more vintage feel to the final images. This unpredictable nature can be part of the appeal, creating one-of-a-kind results. Some photographers enjoy the novelty of shooting a film that is older than themselves and for others it a chance to shoot a film stock that is no longer manufactured.
